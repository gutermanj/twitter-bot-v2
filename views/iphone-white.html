Frequently Asked Questions

How does Automated Fav Trading work?

We've built some pretty complex algorithms to ensure we exceed expectations. We sort your account based on impressions. From there it goes into a pool with similar sized accounts. It then trades 24/7 (unless specified otherwise) with the other accounts in the pool. We guarantee 60+ trades a day which means your impressions will be through the roof.

Do you really guarantee millions of impressions?

If we do not reach the impression count you have paid for we will refund 25% and continue going until we do so.

Can I get a refund?

If it is your first time using one of our monthly services we will offer a full refund as long as it's been under 7 days. After the first week we will no longer process refunds. If you do for some reason decide you'd like a refund you will be permanently blacklisted.

Are my social media accounts at risk of being stolen or banned?

All of our programs are always carefully built within the guidelines of the platform it's being used on. Your accounts security is our number one priority. Your accounts will never be saved on our end, it is simply to log in and then forgot about.

What does your build a brand service offer?

It would be easier to explain what we don't offer, but since that's not what you asked.. We can do anything from designing your logo, getting your website up and running, filing for your LLC or corporation, and all of the fun stuff in between.
